Seaside Dining

Licensed seaside dining at Sunny's @ Moffat. We are children friendly, wheelchair accessible and able to cater to specific dietary requirements. Heated in winter, air conditioned in summer with an al fresco dining are looking over Moffat Beach. Please note menus are only samples and may not be what we are currently serving.



Moffat Beach, there is definitely something about this picture perfect location that just keeps people coming back. And no wonder with Sunny's @ Moffat's wonderful food combined with the perfect weather and our stunning views that can be seen from every table.

Head chef, owner and Moffat Beach local, Sunny Moore has a passion for cooking and combined with stunning location keeps it alive all year round.
Only the freshest, local produce is used with ingredients sourced from around the Sunshine Coast. The menu changes seasonally with old favourites remaining for our

loyal customers that continue to return.

With options covering big breakfasts, to lazy long lunches and delicious dinners there is always something to entice. The specials board constantly changes and gives the customers an insight into upcoming menu changes.

Sunny's @ Moffat is a family run business that loves to get to know you the customer just as well as we know all our staff members.
We encourage that warm, comfortable, laid back atmosphere, that we love to work in and we know you enjoy.
