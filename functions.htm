Weddings and Functions

We pride ourselves on throwing a great party! Since opening we have catered for quite a vew different events; weddings, engagements, staff get togethers, Christmas parties, birthdays, anniversaries, the list goes on!!!

 

We can cater to any style of event and liase with you until we meet every detail. Sunny's can organise a sit down dinner for a maximum capacity of 110 guests, with guests using our indoor and outdoor areas for functions of this size.

For smaller parties you may choose to use only our indoor or our outdoor area depending on numbers. We can also cater for finger food, stand up cocktail parties with a maximum of 150 guests.

A room charge is sometimes applicable if you are using a party planner who is bringing a lot of extra furniture etc, or if you wish to book the entire venue for a smaller party, or if you require us to organise extras for your event.

 

Sunny's can organise several different food options. Our finger food packages are based on a minimum of 20 people at an hourly rate for a minimum of 2 hours.
Our sit down dinners are alternate drop with a three dish choice maximum for groups over 30 people. You can choose which options and how many options you like from;

breads, entrees, finger food, mains and desserts. Your entire menu can be discussed when you sit down with Sunny to organise your event. Smaller groups may choose to order directly off the menu or have a reduced choice menu organised.
